Famous Inventors

Explore the thinkers and inventors who changed the world.

Physics & Engineering Legends

Isaac Newton

English mathematician, physicist; laws of motion & gravity.

Nikola Tesla

Serbian-American inventor; AC electricity and radio innovations.

Thomas Edison

Prolific inventor; practical electric light bulb & phonograph.

Wright Brothers

Built the first powered airplane (Orville & Wilbur).

Galileo Galilei

Astronomer and physicist; improvements to telescope and heliocentrism.

Richard Feynman

Theoretical physicist known for quantum electrodynamics and teaching.

Alexander Graham Bell

Inventor,Scientist and Engineer who built the telephone.

Tech & Computing Innovators

Ada Lovelace

Considered the first computer programmer for work on the Analytical Engine.

Tim Berners-Lee

Inventor of the World Wide Web.

Steve Jobs

Co-founder of Apple; revolutionized personal devices and design.

Science & Discovery Heroes

Marie Curie

Physicist/chemist; research on radioactivity, discovered radium & polonium.

Louis Pasteur

Microbiologist; pasteurization and germ theory pioneer.

Benjamin Franklin

Founding father and inventor (lightning rod, bifocals).

Renaissance & Visionary Thinkers

Leonardo da Vinci

Renaissance polymath who designed early machines and studies of flight.
