Steve Jobs

Born: 1955 | Died: 2011

Co-founder of Apple; revolutionized personal devices and design.

Major contributions

  • Apple I & II influence
  • iPhone revolution
  • Design & UX emphasis

Biography

Early Life

Born in 1955 in San Francisco, Jobs was adopted by Paul and Clara Jobs. From a young age, he showed interest in electronics and design, later co-founding Apple with Steve Wozniak in his garage.

Scientific Work

Jobs transformed technology with products like the Macintosh, iPod, iPhone, and iPad, blending innovation with elegant design and usability.

Legacy

His vision reshaped the digital world, influencing computing, music, and communication industries. Jobs remains an icon of creativity and entrepreneurship.
