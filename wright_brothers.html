Wright Brothers

Born: 1871/1874 | Died: 1948/1949

Built the first powered airplane (Orville & Wilbur).

Major contributions

  • First controlled powered flight
  • Aerodynamic studies

Biography

Early Life

Orville and Wilbur Wright were born in the late 19th century in Dayton, Ohio. Fascinated by mechanics, they operated a bicycle business that financed their aeronautical experiments.

Scientific Work

In 1903, they achieved the first controlled, sustained flight in a powered aircraft. Their methodical testing and wind-tunnel research established key principles of aerodynamics.
